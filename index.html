About Me

I'm Tom Dror (she/her), a postdoctoral researcher at CIRES, University of Colorado Boulder, embedded in the NOAA Chemical Sciences Laboratory, Clouds, Aerosol, and Climate Group. My passion for clouds drives my research, which focuses on the coupling between land and clouds, largely in the tropics and the Amazon forest. For these investigations, my primary tools are measurements of the Earth's atmosphere, especially those from satellites.
